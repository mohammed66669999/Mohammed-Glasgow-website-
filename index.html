Glasgow: A City Full of Character

Glasgow is one of my favourite cities because it has a unique mix of history, culture, and friendly people. The city is known for its beautiful Victorian architecture, lively music scene, and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you are walking through the busy streets of the city centre or relaxing by the River Clyde, Glasgow always feels full of energy.
